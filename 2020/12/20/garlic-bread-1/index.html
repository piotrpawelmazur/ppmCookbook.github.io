Garlic Bread One

Dec 20, 2020
1 minute read
  • 1 loaf French or Italian bread
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter. softened
  • 2 garlic cloves (small)
  • 1 tsp salt

Preheat oven to 400 degrees with rack in the top position. Slice bread in half horizontally. In a small bowl, mix together butter, garlic, and salt. Spread seasoned butter on a cut side of both bread halves.

Reclose halves to form a sandwich, and wrap in aluminium foil. Bake 10 minutes. Switch oven settings to broil. Remove bread from oven, and remove foil.

Lay bread halves, cut side up, on a baking sheet. Place under broiler untill butter bubbles and edges of bread start to turn brown, about 30 seconds. Let cool slightly, then slice into serving pieces.

Bake: 400 F / 15 min
